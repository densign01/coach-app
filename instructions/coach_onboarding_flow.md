# Coach — Onboarding Flow (Chat-Driven)

The onboarding process is structured as a **conversational chat** with the Coach. Each step is a question or prompt; responses are stored in the user profile. The flow is adaptive: skip/branch based on responses.

---

## 1. Welcome & Context
- **Coach:** "Welcome! I’ll ask a few quick questions to get to know you and tailor your plan."

---

## 2. Basics
1. Age
   - "How old are you?"
2. Sex
   - "What’s your sex (male, female, other)?"
3. Height
   - "How tall are you?"
4. Weight
   - "What do you currently weigh?"
5. Activity baseline
   - "How many steps do you usually get on a typical workday?"
   - "Do you wear a watch/track steps?"

---

## 3. Health & Constraints
1. Existing injuries or limitations
   - "Do you have any injuries or conditions (e.g., back pain, GERD) I should keep in mind?"
2. Sleep
   - "How many hours of sleep do you typically get?"
3. Current medications/supplements (optional)
   - "Do you take any medications or supplements relevant to fitness/nutrition?"

---

## 4. Goals
1. Weight goals
   - "What’s your target weight range, if you have one?"
   - "How quickly do you want to get there?"
2. Fitness goals
   - "Do you want to focus more on cardio, strength, or overall health?"
   - "Are there any sports/events you’re training for?"
3. Nutrition goals
   - "Are you mainly interested in weight loss, muscle gain, eating healthier, or something else?"

---

## 5. Current Habits
1. Meals
   - "Walk me through a typical day of eating (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks)."
2. Exercise
   - "How many days a week do you currently exercise, and what type of workouts do you usually do?"
3. Alcohol
   - "Do you drink alcohol? If so, how often?"
4. Dessert/sweets
   - "Do you usually have dessert or sweets?"

---

## 6. Preferences
1. Dietary preferences
   - "Any dietary restrictions (vegetarian, kosher, allergies)?"
2. Food dislikes
   - "Any foods you really dislike or don’t want in your plan?"
3. Workout preferences
   - "Do you prefer gym workouts, home workouts, classes, outdoor activities, or a mix?"

---

## 7. Motivation & Support
1. Motivation source
   - "What’s motivating you to work with me right now?"
2. Support system
   - "Do you have a support system (family, friends, trainer) or should I assume it’s just you and me?"

---

## 8. Wrap-Up
- **Coach:** "Thanks! I’ll use this info to set your initial nutrition targets and workout plan. You can always update me if things change."
- Store profile data into user record.
